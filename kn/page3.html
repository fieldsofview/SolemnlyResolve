The first step of the game is to determine a

starting scenario, which will be generated randomly.

The starting scenario will shape the status quo of the country, described through 4 States of the Nation.

Options may be chosen in 2 ways; first being,


All characters come to a consensus and choose

one of the options by clicking on 'Consensus'.

In each turn, you will be presented with a situation.

Go through it carefully. Discuss and decide your position

on the matter based on the options provided. The chosen option for each situation will have an impact on the 4 States of the Nation and the Preamble that will be created.

In case two or more characters are willing to pay 3 Tokens to impose different options, the character who agrees to pay more Tokens will prevail over all other choices.

Once the starting scenario is chosen, you will be randomly assigned a Character Card. You have to

meet and maintain the targets for your character till

the game ends. Each character will receive 10 Tokens to spend within the game.

OR, The second way being,

A character may unilaterally impose their opinion by selecting the option of their choice by clicking on the 'Give' button. The character must spend 3 Tokens to do so.
